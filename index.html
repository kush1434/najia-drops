Who We Are

Naija Drops is a nonprofit focused on delivering clean, safe water to families in Imo State, Nigeria. We partner with local leaders, engineers, and volunteers to build wells and train communities on upkeep.

What We Do

We raise funds, build wells, and ensure sustainability through community training.
