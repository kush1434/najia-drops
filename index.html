Who We Are

Naija Drops is a community-driven nonprofit focused on delivering clean, safe water to families across Imo State, Nigeria. We partner with local leaders, engineers, and volunteers to identify need, drill wells, and train communities on upkeep.

Transparency guides everything we do—every project is documented with photos, GPS coordinates, and maintenance plans so donors can see their impact.

Our Approach

  • Community-first site selection with local councils
  • Durable solutions: boreholes, hand pumps, and solar-powered wells
  • Local training for maintenance & parts sourcing
  • Public dashboards for funds, timelines, and outcomes

What We Do

1) Raise

We mobilize donations from individuals, schools, and partners to fund wells for high-need communities.

2) Build

With local engineers, we drill, install pumps, and construct protective aprons and drainage for safe, reliable access.

3) Sustain

Community water committees manage maintenance, contributions, and hygiene education for long-term impact.
